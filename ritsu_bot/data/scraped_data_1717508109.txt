If your track will be changed, have your coordinator to submit a new nomination form, It is not possible to use the email address which is already registered, so inform your coordinator a different e-mail address of yours.<br><span class=wysiwyg-underline>For self-sponsored students</span>: Please use a different email address than the one you are already registered with and submit a new application form.</p><p><br><strong>・Is it possible for SKP students to take courses at campuses other than which they are registered at? The IJL track is an Intensive Japanese Learning track and requires a lot of review as well as preparing for lessons. so, as a general rule,<span style="color: #c00000;"> <span class=wysiwyg-color-black>IJL students may register for a maximum of two (2) English courses.</span></span></p><p><span class=wysiwyg-underline>Regular Courses in English （RCE ）track/Regular Courses in Japanese （RCJ） track students:</span><br>Please note that in general, students who are registered at the campus will receive the first priority to register for courses offered at that campus.</p><p><span class=wysiwyg-underline>Notes:</span><br>For courses that have limited capacity, if the applicants exceed the capacity limit, a lottery will be held. Please understand that both the JASSO and RUSSES scholarships are highly competitive, and applicants may not be selected for either scholarship.</p><p> </p><p><strong>・I have been selected to receive a scholarship of more than 80,000 yen per month, which was not determined at the time of application. If there are any changes, you will need to reapply for a COE, so please make sure to fill out the statement correctly.</p><p>&lt;If you do not have a passport&gt;<br>Please submit a copy of your new passport application or a statement.
