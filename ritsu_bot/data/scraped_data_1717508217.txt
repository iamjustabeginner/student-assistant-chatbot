<div class=main-contents><div class="column-group is-one"><div class=column-block><h2 class="mod-title type2">International Student Dormitory</h2><p><img alt="OIC International House" src="/image.jsp?id=372189&version=English"><br></p><div class=mod-entry></div><h3 class="mod-title type3">OIC International House</h3><div class=mod-wysiwyg><p>In March 2018, the OIC International House (OIC I-House) opened near the Ritsumeikan University Osaka Ibaraki Campus. <br></p><p>OIC I-House provides various types of support to help international students adjust to life and customs in Japan, in addition to being an environment that promotes international/intercultural exchange, diverse learning experiences, and community involvement through shared dormitory life with both international and Japanese students. <br></p><p>By living in the dormitory and participating in its various activities, OIC I-House residents are able to cultivate skills such as intercultural understanding, listening skills, and foreign language ability. <br></p><p></p></div><h4 class="mod-title type4">Features</h4><div class=mod-entry><div class=l-frame><div class=mod-wysiwyg><p>・All share-type rooms, with 6 people living in 1 unit (individual bedrooms)<br> ・Resident Mentors (RMs) <br> ・Building manager available during the day<br> ・Auto-lock entrance <br> ・Located approximately 18 minutes away from campus on foot (approximately 10 minutes by bicycle) </p><p>*<a href=https://global.support.ritsumei.ac.jp/hc/ja/articles/29210314620691-%E5%9B%BD%E9%9A%9B%E5%AF%AE-%E3%83%AC%E3%82%B8%E3%83%87%E3%83%B3%E3%83%88%E3%83%A1%E3%83%B3%E3%82%BF%E3%83%BC-RM-Resident-Mentor title=RM（レジデントメンター）募集ページ>RMs</a>: Residents who work with the university and building managers to ensure that OIC I-House residents can have a safe and comfortable dormitory experience by providing support to residents and encouraging exchange;
