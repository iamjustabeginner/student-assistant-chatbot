<div class=article-body><p>Even if you have a chronic illness (or an injury or illness currently being treated), you can still apply to study abroad programs.<br>However, after you decide to participate in a study abroad program, you must report your health condition. Based on your declared information, the school doctor at the Medical Service Center will check whether your health condition allows you to study abroad.<br>If necessary, the school doctor may communicate with participants or their doctors.</p><p> </p><p>*For inquiries, please contact the study abroad staff at the International Center <a href="https://global.support.ritsumei.ac.jp/hc/en/requests/new?ticket_form_id=360006493753">＜Here＞</a>.</p></div>
