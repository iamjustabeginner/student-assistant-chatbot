(Please refer to the academic calendar for the detailed schedule of the makeup classes.)<br> Makeup classes format will be either face-to-face classes, media-based classes (live-streaming), or media-based classes (on-demand). This may differ from the normal implementation format.<br></p><p>* In the event that classes cannot be held due to a disaster, etc., an established makeup class day may be scheduled on a day other than those specified in the academic calendar.<br> * The makeup class may be set on other than the established makeup class days after guaranteeing students the opportunity to take the class.<br> * Exams, quizzes, experiments, and important presentations may be given after guaranteeing students the opportunity to attend and take the tests (including web-based delivery). <br> * If a makeup class overlaps on the established makeup class day, please attend one of the classes at your own discretion.</p><br><p>You can get the latest information on the courses you are registered for (cancelled classes, makeup classes, classroom changes, etc.) through the Course News on manaba+R.</p><br><p>For information on the courses which are in the process of being registered and are not displayed at “My Courses” on “My page”, please click on the Course News “List all”.You will then see the “All Cancellations, Makeups, Classroom Changes”; please click on it to check the details.</p><!-- #cons02 --><section class="section u-mt40" id=cons02><h3>How Class Attendance is taken</h3><p>How student attendance is taken varies depending on the instructor.
