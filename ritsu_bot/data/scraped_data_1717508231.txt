<div class="main-contents type1 type4"><div class="column-group is-one"><div class=column-block><h2 class="mod-title type2" id=page-title1>International Student Support Coordinator</h2><div class=mod-wysiwyg><p>The Kinugasa and BKC International Centers will begin offering consultation services provided by an International Student Support Coordinator stationed at each center respectively. The OIC International Center will offer the same consultation services but without designating a specific International Student Support Coordinator at this time.</p><p><strong>Services offered</strong><br> The International Student Support Coordinators handle a variety of inquiries related to international students’ student life. International students can visit them to voice any concerns, questions, or worries they may have, and the coordinator will offer advice and assistance, including liaising between the international student and other on and off-campus facilities if necessary to try and reach a solution.<br> [Examples of issues fielded by the International Student Support Coordinator]<br></p><ul><li> “I don’t understand my courses and can’t earn any credits” (curriculum questions)</li><li> “I can’t make friends/ I can’t get along well with others/ I’m feeling down/ I can’t manage my time effectively/ etc…” (questions about relationships, health and daily life)</li><li> “I want to participate in an extracurricular activity/ I want to ask about balancing extracurricular activities and part-time work” (extracurricular questions)</li><li> Issues related to incidents, accidents and trouble (urgent questions)</li><li> Questions and concerns where a student doesn’t know where to turn and who to talk to, etc…</li></ul><p></p><p><strong>Consultation Hours</strong><br> Consultations can be made from Friday, May 10th, 2019 at 10:00.<br> -Mon, Tue, Thu, Fri: 10:00~11:30, 12:30~17:00<br> -Wed: 12:30-17:00 on Wednesdays<br><i>*From 13:00-17:00 during the long vacations</i><br></p><p><strong>Place</strong><br> Kinugasa International Center<br> BKC International Center<br> (OIC International Center is scheduled to designate an on-site International Student Support Coordinator from Fall 2019.
