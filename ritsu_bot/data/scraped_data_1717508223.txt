If the student fails to take the procedures during the period when classes are held, it will be difficult to receive academic guidance and support, therefore, the student should complete the procedures by the end of July for Spring Semester and by the end of January for Fall Semester. <br> Please check below for details on special considerations for classes.<br><a href=https://www.ritsumei.ac.jp/pathways-future/eng/course/absence.html/#cons04e>https://www.ritsumei.ac.jp/pathways-future/eng/course/absence.html/#cons04e</a></p><p><strong>（1）When a student has infected COVID-19</strong><br> 　　　* Please process as soon as possible after your diagnosis.</p><p><strong></strong></p><p><strong>（2）When a student has infected seasonal influenza</strong><br> 　　　* Please process as soon as possible after your diagnosis. </p><p><strong></strong></p><strong></strong><p><strong>（3）When a student has infected school infectious diseases other than the above</strong><br> 　　　*Please process after obtaining Certificate of Recovery from Infectious Disease filled in by the medical institution.</p><br><h4>＜How to proceed＞</h4><p>① Please complete the procedures through the <a href=https://www.ritsumei.ac.jp/health/ >Medical Service Center website</a> if you have contracted school infectious disease. </p><p>② The Medical Service Center will issue the "Confirmation of Suspension Period due to Infectious Disease" and send it to the student’s Ritsumeikan e-mail address.<br></p><p>③ Please submit the issued application form promptly through manaba+R &gt; Applicable Course &gt; “Tutoring Collections". However, if you receive any individual instructions given by the instructor, please follow it. If it is difficult to do that (e.g., the student does not have the contact information of the instructor, etc.), please consult with the Manabi Station of affiliated campus (or the Administrative Office of Inter-Faculty Graduate Schools for Suzaku campus).</p><br></div><div class=column-block><h2 class="mod-title type2" id=page-title1>Special Measures in the Case of Suspension from Final Examination</h2><div class=mod-wysiwyg><p>In cases where the student was not able to take final examinations due to suspension from classes, the student will be able to apply for make-up examinations in accordance with the "Ritsumeikan University Final Examination Regulations. For details of the application procedures, please check the following.</p><p><a href=https://www.ritsumei.ac.jp/pathways-future/eng/examinations_grades/makeup_examinations.html/ >https://www.ritsumei.ac.jp/pathways-future/eng/examinations_grades/makeup_examinations.html/</a><br></p></div><div class=mod-wysiwyg></div><h2 class="mod-title type2" id=page-title1>Notes</h2><div class=mod-wysiwyg><p> （１）Should a student contract one of the infectious diseases listed below, it is necessary that measures be taken to prevent its spread. Therefore, infected students are obligated to report their infected status, as specified in the “School Health and Safety Act” to the administrative office of their college or graduate school at the point their diagnosis is confirmed. The infectious diseases requiring disclosure listed below have been determined pursuant to the Ministry of Health, Labour and Welfare's rules concerning "Notifiable Infectious Diseases."</p><ul style="margin-left: 16pt;"><li>Type 1 Infectious Diseases: Ebola hemorrhagic fever, plague, Crimean–Congo hemorrhagic fever, Marburg virus disease, smallpox, Lassa fever, South American hemorrhagic fever, Polio, diphtheria, SARS (Sever Acute Respiratory Syndrome), avian influenza, new strains of influenza, other designated infectious diseases etc.</li><li>Other Infectious Diseases: measles, rubella, tuberculosis, bacterial meningitis, cholera, shigellosis (bacillary dysentery), EHEC (Enterohemorrhagic Escherichia coli), typhoid fever, salmonella.</li></ul></div><div class=mod-wysiwyg><p>（２）In the interest of preserving the health and safety of students, faculty and other staff, and in order to prevent the spread of disease, it may be determined that an infected student shall take official absence. In such a case, the student will be informed of the subsequent procedures.</p></div></div></div></div></div></div></div>
