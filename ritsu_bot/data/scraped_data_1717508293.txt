Therefore, we encourage students to take advantage of this system.</p><p>For detailed information such as registering procedures and subjects available, please see the information from the URL below. </p><p><a href=https://www.ritsumei.ac.jp/ru_gr/g-career/stepup/common.html/ target=_blank>https://www.ritsumei.ac.jp/ru_gr/g-career/stepup/common.html/</a><span style="font-size: 10pt;">(Japanese text only)</span></p><p>*The system is available for students in Professional Degree Program (excluding the School of Law), Master’s Program, Doctoral Program and Integrated Doctoral Program. </p><div class="bgBox__gray u-mt30 u-mb60"><p><strong>For inquiries</strong><br> Office of Graduate Studies (Kinugasa)<br></p></div><h4>Kansai Four-University Consortium Program (credit transfer system)</h4><p>The inter-graduate school credit transfer system among four universities in Kansai is a credit transfer system established and operated based on an agreement made among four universities in the Kansai area: Ritsumeikan University, Doshisha University, Kansai University and Kwansei Gakuin University. <br> Based on this agreement, all four universities accept non-degree students who join this Consortium Program from other three universities and allow them to take courses offered at their graduate schools and earn credits. <br> Students are able to acquire knowledge other skills that are difficult to acquire at their own universities. </p><p>For detailed information such as registering procedures and subjects available, please see the information from the URL below.</p><p><a href=https://www.ritsumei.ac.jp/ru_gr/g-career/stepup/kansai.html/ target=_blank>https://www.ritsumei.ac.jp/ru_gr/g-career/stepup/kansai.html/</a><span style="font-size: 10pt;">(Japanese text only)</span></p><div class="bgBox__gray u-mt30 u-mb60"><p><strong>For inquiries</strong><br> KIC Manabi Station<br></p></div><!-- 内容ここまで --></div></div></div></section></article></main>
