<div class=article-body><p>The tuition reduction will be applied only for the semester in which the application is submitted and/or the following semester. In order to be eligible for the tuition reduction, your enrollment status must be registered as "current student (zaigaku)" or "study abroad (ryuugaku)".</p><p>If you take a leave of absence, the tuition reduction will not be applied to that semester, nor will it be deferred.</p></div>
