<main class=l-main role=main><article><section class=p-catch><div class=p-catch__inner><h1 class=p-catch__pageTitle>Purchase of a Student Commuter Pass</h1></div></section><section class=p-body><div class=pageArticle><div class=p-chapter><div class=p-chapter__inner><!-- 内容ここから --><h4>Purpose of use</h4><p>A student Commuter Pass is limited to use only for the purpose of commuting to campus.</p><p>*Students cannot purchase the student commuter pass for using public transportation to participate in a club activity, work part-time or go job hunting.</p><h4>Allowable distance</h4><p>Students can purchase the student commuter pass for the section between the current home address and the nearest station to your home campus.</p><p>*Specify your current home address and the commuting route on the enrollment confirmation label on your student ID card.</p><p>For the nearest stations please check the following site:</p><p><a href=https://www.ritsumei.ac.jp/pathways-future/eng/student_life/certificate.html/ target=_blank>https://www.ritsumei.ac.jp/infostudents/certificate/</a></p><h4>Required items</h4><p>When purchasing the student commuter pass, students must present their student ID card.</p><p>*You cannot purchase a student commuter pass unless the sticker for the academic year in question is not attached to the bottom of the enrollment confirmation label on the back of your student ID card and your current home address and commuting route are specified on the label.<br> (The enrollment confirmation label on the back side functions as a student commuter certificate)</p><h4>Important points to note</h4><p>・If you use more than two transportation agencies, specify each portion of the route.</p><p>・If the space on your ID card for providing information on your commuting route is insufficient, you should contact Manabi Station.</p><p>・If you change your address, you need to have your student ID card stamped with a correction seal by the University. Please follow the procedures at Manabi Station.</p><div class="bgBox__gray u-mt30 u-mb60"><p><strong>For inquiries</strong><br> Kinugasa: Manabi Station（Gakujikan Hall 1F）<br> BKC: Manabi Station（Prism House 1F）<br> OIC: Manabi Station（Building A 1F AC Administrative Office）<br> Suzaku: Administrative Office, Inter-Faculty Graduate Schools<br></p></div><!-- 内容ここまで --></div></div></div></section></article></main>
