By doing so, you can avoid being unwittingly involved in ethical misconduct.</p><p>Ritsumeikan University has been educating students on research ethics through guidance by research supervisors, regular courses of studies, and extracurricular seminars, while reminding students of risks related to research misconduct, such as falsification and plagiarism, etc. at orientations for new students.
