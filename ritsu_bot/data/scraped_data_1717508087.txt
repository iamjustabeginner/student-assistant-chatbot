Receive a new Residence Card </h3><p class=wysiwyg-indent2><span class=wysiwyg-color-black>Once received the postcard from the Immigration Bureau notifying you of the result of your application for an extension of period of stay, go pick up your new Residence Card.</span></p><p class=wysiwyg-indent2> </p><p class=wysiwyg-indent2><span style="color: black;">■Required documents for in-person pick up </span></p><p class=wysiwyg-indent2><span class=wysiwyg-color-black>Bring the documents listed on the postcard. </span></p><p class=wysiwyg-indent2><span class=wysiwyg-color-black>You also need to pay using 4000 Yen revenue stamp, which can be purchased at post offices and some convenience stores. </span></p><p class=wysiwyg-indent2> </p><p class=wysiwyg-indent2><span style="color: black;">■For those who have or plan to have a part-time job</span></p><p class=wysiwyg-indent2><span style="color: black;">Be sure to re-apply for “permission to engage in activity other than that permitted under the status of residence previously granted” when picking up a new Residence Card. </span></p><p> </p><h3 id=h_01HARSJA470726WJDQ79AYMJ0Z>5.
