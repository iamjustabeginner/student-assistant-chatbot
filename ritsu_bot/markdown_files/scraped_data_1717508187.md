Question: I have VELC and CASEC language proficiency scores, but do I also need TOEIC or TOEFL scores?

Answer:
The English proficiency test required for application differs depending on the program. If your score for one of the English exams listed in the application guidelines meets the requirements, you can apply. For details, please check the application guidelines for each program.

*For inquiries, please contact the study abroad staff at the International Center.
